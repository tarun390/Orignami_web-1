Interesting Facts About Brown-Bear:-

  • There are around 180,000 to 200,000 brown bears worldwide. .
  • The brown bear can reach a weight of between 150 and 370 kilogrammes depending on age, sex and season.
  • Despite their weight, the animals can cover short distances at speeds of up to 50 km/h.
  • Brown bears are generally loners who will only seek a mate for short periods.
  • At birth, bear cubs are blind and naked.

Interesting Facts About Panda-Bear:-

  • The giant panda’s having black and white coat is one of the shyest and rarest animals in the world. They are one of the best-known species..
  • During Winter season, the giant pandas don’t hibernate but take shelter in caves and hollow trees.
  • They also come under endangered species of the world. There are only about 1,000 giant pandas left in the wild..
  • They used to resemble a bear but in fact, they have a strong liking towards racoons.
  • It is believed that pandas have lived on Earth for two to three million years..

Interesting Facts About Cicada:-

  • Cicadas are members of the Cicadoidea superfamily.
  • There are about 3,000 known species of cicadas worldwide.
  • A cicada’s body color can be black, brown and green.
  • Cicadas are separated into two classifications, annual cicadas and periodic cicadas.
  • Cicada species vary in size, between 0.75 of an inch and 2.25 inches.
